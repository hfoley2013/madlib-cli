Make Me A Video Game!

I the blue and black Harper have ran Neil's scared sister and plan to steal her purple boat!

What are a bear and backpacking chicken to do? Before you can help Jenny, you'll have to collect the orange cars and loud headphones that open up the 40 worlds connected to A Lili's Lair. There are 3 computers and 2 desks in the game, along with hundreds of other goodies for you to find.
