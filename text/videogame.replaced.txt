Make Me A Video Game!

I the blue and red Jerk have ran Scott's shiney sister and plan to steal her loud boats!

What are a bear and backpacking chicken to do? Before you can help Jennifer, you'll have to collect the purple apples and juicy oranges that open up the 45 worlds connected to A Harry's Lair. There are 10 broom and 10 avacado in the game, along with hundreds of other goodies for you to find.
